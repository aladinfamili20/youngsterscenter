About Us

Our Non Profit

Young people are typically defined solely by their challenges, not by their potential. They urgently need, and aspire to earn, knowledge, training, and opportunities that lead to long-term professional and personal success.

ALL ABOUT YOUNGSTERS CENTER

YOUNGSTERS CENTER is a non-profit organisation, and shall operate exclusively for reaching the young people and adults to develop important interpersonal skills, knowledge, and careers in community.With comprehensive vision, mission and objectives to work in partnership with churches, associations, communities based organization (C.B.O), Both National and International Organization and Donors agencies with free will donate from government, individual and from corporate Bodies

Action

Organization will try to do many different things in order to attain these goals. For example, It will hold a weekly "Combination" meeting where it will bring together diverse groups of young people to explore cultural, racial, and sexual issues. But also it will be involved in many aspects of youth's lives, and offers opportunities for leadership development and guidance for both education and careers. Young people will transform feelings and experiences about youth issues into music, dance, and drama. They will perform their skits in their neighborhoods and at schools, community centers, and prisons. They will perform on stage, and some of their works will be developed into videos and shown on television to send a positive message to youths throughout the community.

Vision

Creating a world where all young people are seen for their potential, and getting power.

MISSION

Empowering diverse young people to positively impact the world.

Our goals:

-Having a safe environment for youths

-Promoting open communication for youths themselves.

-Multicultural center

-Enhancing leadership development among young people

COMMUNITY

-Y.C will help change public perception of young people from being "the problem" to an important part of making life better in the community.

-Y.C will help young people how to involve in the community and make them see themselves as problem solvers; they will really do become less likely to cause problems and more likely to want to look for solutions.

-Y.C will develop the young people's leadership potential, so that as they will grow and learn they will become thoughtful, prepared leaders of the society.
