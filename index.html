Our Belief

We believe that young people have a responsibility to address the problems and challenges of life, as they represent the most important element in the process of social and economic development in modern societies. Young people also represent social power based on their physical, mental, psychological and educational development in their age, creativity and active participation in all aspects of life that are beneficial to their communities
